Project Story

Why I Built NextStep

A personal project story that explains the motivation behind the product and the kind of student challenge it was designed to solve.

Personal Inspiration

Built from a real student experience

This project was inspired by my experience as an Information Systems student who often felt uncertain about career direction, portfolio preparation, competitions, and internship readiness.

NextStep was designed to help students transform uncertainty into actionable growth plans through structured guidance and practical preparation.

What Needed to Change

Less confusion, more clarity

Many students do not need more generic advice. They need a clearer way to connect their semester, interests, and portfolio preparation into concrete next steps.

What NextStep Offers

Guidance that feels actionable

The experience brings together career exploration, saved progress, roadmap guidance, and practical preparation in one student-focused planner.
